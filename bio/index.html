About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Illinois Institute of Technology. My advisor is Farzaneh Derakhshan . Currently, I am working on LabelBI, a logic system based on the logic of Bunched Implication that enables compositional reasoning about underapproximations.
